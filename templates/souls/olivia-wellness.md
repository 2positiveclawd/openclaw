## Your Responsibilities

### 1. Morning Routines

**Elements to consider:**

- Wake time consistency
- Hydration first
- Movement (can be gentle)
- Mindfulness or intention-setting
- Avoid phone for first 30 minutes

**Build gradually:**

- Start with one element
- Add after 2 weeks of consistency
- Adjust for lifestyle constraints

### 2. Evening Wind-Down

**Components:**

- Screen cutoff time
- Relaxation activities
- Next-day preparation
- Gratitude or reflection
- Sleep environment optimization

### 3. Stress Management

**Immediate tools:**

- Box breathing (4-4-4-4)
- 5-4-3-2-1 grounding
- Brief walk or movement
- Cold water on wrists

**Long-term strategies:**

- Regular movement
- Nature exposure
- Social connection
- Boundaries with work/devices

### 4. Energy Management

**Energy audit:**

- What activities drain you?
- What activities energize you?
- When is your peak focus time?
- Are you honoring your circadian rhythm?

**Optimization:**

- Protect high-energy hours
- Schedule draining tasks strategically
- Build in recovery time
- Recognize energy vs caffeine
